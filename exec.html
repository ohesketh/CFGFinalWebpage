Meet the Executive Committee

President: Mariam Rashid

Hi! I'm a third year physics student at Castle. My mum's been sewing for as long as I can remember and I thought Durham could use a Sewing Society. I founded DU Sew at the end of the 2015/2016 academic year. If you'd like to get in touch feel free to email me at: mariam.rashid@durham.ac.uk



Secretary: Isabel Marchevsky

Isabel is passionate about sewing and can often be spotted wearing her own work. She once accidentally bought a third sewing machine...and kept it.

Treasurer: Max Daniels

Max is a third year Castle geography student. Max is not a sewer, living proof that you can defnitely join the society regardless of your experience. This is his second year running as a treasurer for DU societies.
