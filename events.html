Our Events

Our events run from 2pm to 4pm every Sunday in the DSU. We're in Upper Kingsgate, which is the room up the stairs opposite the reception.

Pillow Making!

Date: Alongside all other sessions

The DSU has commisioned us to brighten up some of the (ugly) benches in the building! We aim to make 15 pillow covers for square, circular and bolster pillows (but you're welcome to be creative with shapes and sizes!). This project is perfet for both beginners and advanced sewers - it gives good practie with basic sewing if you choose a simpler shape, and it's a really fun project to get creative with if you're more advanced! You're welcome to work on this at any of our sessions.

Patchwork Quilt!

Date: Alongside all other sessions

We invite all of our members to create a personaliised patch! We'll have a session dedicated to this on the 23rd October but you're welcome to do a patch alongside any of the other workshops. We're aiming to have a large patchwork piece at the end of the year built from all your personalised squares.

Halloween Bunting

Date: 23rd October 2016

For our first session of term, we'll be making Halloween Butning. This session is a joint venture with castle sewing society (join their facebook group here, if you're a part of University College)! We'll be providing halloween coloured fabrics and felt, as well teaching from square one. So come along if you've never used a needle in your life or if you're a pro for a fun start to the term!

Plush Toys

Date: 6th November 2016

For our plush toy session, we'll be making plush animals. We'll release a poll on our facebook group before the session to see what animals you're most interested in making, so keep an eye out!

Laptop Sleeves

Date: 13th November 2016

This is planned for the Give it a Go week this November! We'll teach you how to make a snug laptop or tablet sleeve. Please remember to bring the dimensions (length, width and height) of the item you'd like to make the sleeve for.

Relaxed Session

Date: 11th December 2016

Our last session of Michaelmas term will be a relaxed meeting. There will be no structured workshop (which means it's free for non members too) but instead you're welcome to bring any projects you're working on or didn't finish in a previous workshop. Please let us know in advance if you'll need a sewing machine. If you missed a workshop you wanted to attend, we'll bring the spare materials (but in this case, unless you're a member, please bring a £3 contribution towards the materials).

Santa Hats

Date: 4th December 2016

For our Christmas session, we'll be making santa hats. We'll provide red fleece, fur and bells so all you need to bring is your festive cheer! You are of course welcome to bring anything you'd like to add to your hat

Mend And Make Do

Date: TBA

At the start of Epiphany, we'll be running a Mend and Make Do session aimed at helping you fix up those beloved clothes you just can't throw out! We'll have buttons but if you need anything in particular (e.g. a zipper) then please let us know in advance!
